Skool Video Downloader is a browser extension for saving authorized Skool classroom, community, and course videos from pages you can access.

This guide follows the browser extension demo and uses verified repository and release names.

> Only download Skool videos you own, have permission to save, or are legally allowed to access offline.

## What Is Skool Video Downloader?

Skool Video Downloader detects supported Skool videos in your browser and provides a download option from the extension panel.

Verified repository:

[serpapps/skool-downloader](https://github.com/serpapps/skool-downloader)

## Before You Start

You will need:

- Google Chrome.
- Access to a Skool classroom or course video.
- The Chrome release file `skool-downloader-chrome.zip`.
- An email address for one-time verification.

## Step 1: Find the Repository

Search Google for:

```text
serpapps skool downloader
```

Open the GitHub repository for `serpapps/skool-downloader`.

## Step 2: Download the Chrome ZIP

Open **Releases** and select the latest release.

For Chrome, download:

```text
skool-downloader-chrome.zip
```

Other verified browser assets include `skool-downloader-edge.zip` and `skool-downloader-brave.zip`.

## Step 3: Extract the ZIP File

Extract `skool-downloader-chrome.zip` after download.

## Step 4: Load It Through Chrome Extensions

Open:

```text
chrome://extensions
```

Enable **Developer mode**, click **Load unpacked**, and choose the extracted folder.

## Step 5: Pin the Extension

Open the Chrome extensions menu and pin Skool Video Downloader to the toolbar.

## Step 6: Verify Your Email

Open the extension, enter your email, click the send verification option, copy the code from your inbox, and verify.

After that, the extension can download from Skool pages you can access.

## Step 7: Open a Skool Classroom Video

Navigate to the Skool classroom or course page with the video.

If the video does not appear in the extension, refresh the page or click play. The walkthrough shows that some Skool videos must be played before they are available to the extension.

## Step 8: Download the Video

Open the extension and click **Download Video** for the detected video.

The progress indicator shows the download status.

## Step 9: Find the File

When the download finishes, the video saves to the download folder configured in your browser.

## Troubleshooting Tips

- Refresh the Skool page if the extension does not detect the video.
- Click play before opening the extension panel.
- Make sure you installed `skool-downloader-chrome.zip`.
- Load the extracted folder, not the ZIP file.

## FAQ

### Why does the video appear only after I click play?

Some Skool players do not load video data into the browser until playback begins.

### Can I use it on different Skool classrooms?

Yes. Open each classroom page, detect the video, and download authorized content.

### Where does the downloaded video go?

It saves wherever Chrome is configured to save downloads.

## Final Thoughts

Install `skool-downloader-chrome.zip`, verify your email, refresh or play the Skool classroom video, then click **Download Video** in the extension.
